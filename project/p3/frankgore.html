Why Frank Gore is One of My Favorite 49ers?

Frank Gore is one of the greatest players to ever suit up for the San Francisco 49ers. As a running back, he was a powerhouse for many years, consistently delivering outstanding performances. I've always enjoy everytime he gets the ball in his hands, and get excited when he breaks for big yardage gain! His resilience and commitment to the game make him one of my all-time favorite 49ers.
