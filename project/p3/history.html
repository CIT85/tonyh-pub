Key Historical Moments

The Start of the Franchise

The San Francisco 49ers were founded in 1946 as part of the All-America Football Conference. In 1950, after the AAFC was disbanded, the 49ers transitioned to the NFL.

First Super Bowl Victory

In 1982, the 49ers won their first Super Bowl under Coach Bill Walsh. Under Bill Walsh, the 49ers transformed into a dominant force in the NFL. With key quarterback Joe Montana, who was named MVP. This win marked the beginning of an amazing era for the 49ers.

Legendary Coaches

Bill Walsh

Bill Walsh retired his pro coaching career after 10 years with a record of 102-62-1, 6 NFC West titles and 3 Super Bowls win.
